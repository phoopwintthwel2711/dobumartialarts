DOBU's History

The history of DoBu Martial Art dates back to 1990 when it was founded by Master Hiroshi Nakamura. Driven by his passion for martial arts and a desire to share its profound teachings, Master Nakamura established Dobu as a place where students could not only learn self-defense techniques but also cultivate inner strength and discipline. Over the years, Dobu Martial Art has evolved into a thriving community, attracting individuals from diverse backgrounds who seek to embark on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. The academy's rich history is a testament to the dedication and vision of its founder, and it continues to inspire and shape the lives of countless individuals on their martial arts path.
