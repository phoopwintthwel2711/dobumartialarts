Facilities

DoBu Martial Arts enhances the overall training experience for members. The combination of a safe and versatile matted area, a fully-equipped gym for comprehensive workouts, relaxing sauna and steam rooms, and convenient changing facilities creates an environment that supports members' physical and mental well-being, encouraging them to achieve their fitness and martial arts goals effectively.

Large and Matted Martial Arts Area

Cushioned surface reduces the risk of injuries. Enhances balance and technique precision. Accommodates various martial arts disciplines.

Fully-Equipped Gym

Wide range of fitness equipment for diverse workouts. Supports martial artists' performance and endurance. Members can customize their routines.

Sauna

Provides a peaceful environment for stress relief. Promotes elimination of toxins through sweating. Aids in post-workout muscle relaxation.

Steam Room

Helps open airways for better breathing. Improves skin hydration and complexion.

Changing

Offers a comfortable space for changing and freshening up. Respects members' privacy with private changing rooms.

Shower

Encourages members to rehydrate post-workout. A well-maintained shower room contributes to an overall positive experience at the gym.

What We Provide

DOBU provides like a martial arts area with mats, a fully furnished gym, a sauna, a steam room, and changing rooms.
